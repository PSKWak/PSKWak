## Three Projects. Three Lessons.

<details>
<summary><b>🧒 Neural Story Generation — The Tradeoff Curve</b></summary>
<br>

Fine-tuned **Flan-T5** using **PEFT/LoRA** to generate children's stories that hit required vocabulary without losing narrative coherence.

The 72% keyword accuracy wasn't the maximum I could achieve.
It was the optimized point where both objectives were acceptably good *simultaneously.*

> **Lesson:** Optimizing a single metric is easy. Knowing which tradeoff to sit on is the real work.

`PyTorch` `LoRA/PEFT` `Flan-T5` `NLP` `Instruction Tuning`

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🚗 Semantic Segmentation — The Honest Metric</b></summary>
<br>

Compared **Vision Transformers** vs **DeepLabV3+** on urban street scenes.
ViT looked fine on pixel accuracy. Boundary F1 told the truth — it collapsed at object edges.
In a city, boundaries are the safety-critical decisions.

> **Lesson:** The metric you choose is a moral decision, not just a technical one.

`DeepLabV3+` `ViT` `mIoU` `Boundary F1` `Computer Vision`

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>⚖️ AI Litigation Platform — The Hidden Column</b></summary>
<br>

Built a platform tracking **8+ legal domains** and **12+ AI copyright cases** with automated CI/CD delivery.
The insight was temporal — lawsuits clustered 12–18 months after model launches.
A right-skewed distribution hiding in a date column most people would have dropped.

> **Lesson:** The insight was hiding in the column everyone ignores.

`Streamlit` `Plotly` `Pandas` `GitHub Actions` `CI/CD`

</details>
